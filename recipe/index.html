Easy Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Eggs
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Graham Cracker Crumbs

Directions

  1. Select pie pan that is about 9 inches in diameter
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  3. Mix butter with graham cracker crumbs, and spread in pan
  4. Put butter in microwave
  5. Mix cream cheese(room temp) with sugar until smooth
  6. Mix in the eggs and vanilla, again until smooth
  7. Pour in pan with graham cracker crust
  8. Bake for about 45 min, then turn oven off and let cool in oven for additional 10 min.
  9. Once cake is cool, cover and put in fridge
  10. Serve in wedges when ready
